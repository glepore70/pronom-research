GIMP Brush, or GIMP Basic Brush, is one of the formats used by the GIMP image editor to store a brush or texture. It is effectively a raster image format.


GIMP Animated Brush
XCF (for a list of other GIMP formats)
There is a third kind of GIMP brush file, GIMP Parametric Brush (.vbr), but it is probably of little interest to applications other than GIMP.

Identifiers
The usual filename extension, at least for current versions of GIMP, is .gbr. Some sources also list .gpb.

Identification
The ASCII characters "GIMP" appear at offset 20.
