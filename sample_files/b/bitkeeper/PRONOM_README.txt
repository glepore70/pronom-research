BitKeeper is a software tool for distributed revision control of computer source code. Originally proprietary software, it was released as open-source software under the Apache-2.0 license on 9 May 2016.[2] BitKeeper is produced by BitMover Inc., a privately held company based in Los Gatos, California[3] and owned by its CEO, Larry McVoy, who had previously designed TeamWare. BitKeeper is no longer developed.[4][5]
