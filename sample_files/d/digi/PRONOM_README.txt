1997-99 Tomasz & Waldemar Piasta

An eight channel tracker for the Amiga created in 1997 by Tomasz and Waldemar Piasta. Later followed by DIGI Booster PRO, much respected by Amiga musicians for its built-in Roland TB303 emulator.
