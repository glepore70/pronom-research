ABBYY, a leading manufacturer of OCR and linguistics software, has announced a new series of electronic dictionaries, ABBYY Lingvo 8.0. The new line will include three products: a new version of the English-Russian-English dictionary ABBYY Lingvo 8.0, a multilingual dictionary ABBYY Lingvo 8.0 Multilingual Edition*, and a version of ABBYY Lingvo 8.0 designed specifically for Pocket PC.
