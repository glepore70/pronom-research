PPT97: Files Installed by the Custom Soundtrack Add-in
Article ID: 196812

Article Last Modified on 7/31/2001


APPLIES TO

Microsoft PowerPoint 97 Standard Edition

This article was previously published under Q196812

SUMMARY
This article lists the files and folders copied to your hard disk when you install the PowerPoint Custom Soundtrack Add-in.

The PowerPoint Custom Soundtrack Add-in is a PowerPoint add-in that can add electronically-generated background music to your slide shows. You can install this add-in from the following folder on the Office 97 compact disc:
<drive>:\ValuPack\MusicTrk


where <drive> represents the drive letter of your CD-ROM drive.

MORE INFORMATION
The following files and folders are installed by the Custom Soundtrack add-in:

<drive:>\Multimedia Files\Music\Interactive Music\ 

where <drive> represents the drive letter of your hard disk.
