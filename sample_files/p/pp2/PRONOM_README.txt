MAGNETIC PAGES PROGRAM
    Magnetic Pages is a software package consisting 
    of 3 programs, that allow you to create and 
    display a disk-based magazine. The magazine 
    produced is of a similar format to that of a 
    traditional paper magazine. You can combine text 
    and graphics on a single page, you can branch to 
    different sections of the magazine by clicking on 
    icons on a page and you can play sound and music.
