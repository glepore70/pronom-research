Beatnik MSB
Beatnik’s Mobile Sound Builder is a
multi-functional content creation tool that
allows developers to compose globally
accepted standard audio content for mobile
devices. With millions of mobile devices
on the market capable of playing scalable
polyphony ringtones, Beatnik’s SP-MIDI
utility helps developers get to market
fast creating revenue-generating content.
Beatnik also enables the creation of
Mobile eXtensible Music Format (Mobile
XMF) content. With our easy-to-use
Mobile XMF builder, mixing SP-MIDI
audio with custom digital samples is
simple, and offers the best combination
of small filesize and custom sounds.
