Game Boy Tracker, originally titled Paragon 5 GameBoy Tracker, is a tracker made for creating music and sound effects for the Game Boy and Game Boy Color. It was designed by the chiptune group Paragon 5 and coded by Stéphane Hockenhull. It was originally designed for Windows, but has since been ported to Linux and Macintosh.
